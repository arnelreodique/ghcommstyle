Here's How It Works

Read through before you start.

  • 1
    40 pairs of statementsEach screen shows two statements. Pick the one that describes you more right now.
  • 2
    Answer based on what is true for youThere are no right or wrong answers. Every pair must be answered before you can see results.
  • 3
    A timer keeps you focusedEach pair has a 60-second countdown. It will not skip you but turns red to remind you not to overthink.
  • 4
    Get your personalised profileAfter all 40 pairs you will see a full breakdown with insights, tips, and a downloadable PDF.
Tip: Pick one context and stick to it - either work or home.
